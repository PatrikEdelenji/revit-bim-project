# BIM Room Quality Rules

## Required Room Metadata

Each room should have:

- Room ID
- Room name
- Floor or level
- Area in square meters
- Volume in cubic meters
- Material information when available
- Window information when available

## Room Naming Rules

Room names should clearly describe the room function, such as:

- Office
- Parking
- Commercial
- Roof
- Corridor
- Technical room

## Quality Review Rules

A room should be manually reviewed if:

- It has unusually large area compared with other rooms.
- It has unusually large volume compared with its area.
- Material is missing or marked as Unknown.
- Window information is missing or always false.
- Room name is too generic or unclear.
